Adaptive strategy / regime-aware risk / open source

It tells you what kind of market
you are in — and trades accordingly.

Each day the system looks at price action and macro data, decides whether you are in a calm bull, choppy, or volatile bear market, and adjusts a systematic trading strategy to fit. The result is meaningfully smaller drawdowns, with statistically defensible numbers behind the claim.

01 / How it works

Three moves, run every day.

The whole pipeline reduces to three steps. Repeat them at market close, every trading day.

01

Observe

Read daily price action and a panel of macro indicators — VIX, yield-curve slope, the dollar, credit spreads. Same inputs a discretionary trader would glance at, written down explicitly.

02

Classify

A hidden-Markov model assigns the day to one of three regimes — calm bull, choppy, or volatile bear — with a probability for each. The states emerge from the data; you do not hand-pick them.

03

Adapt

A trend-following strategy then scales its position size by the regime. Full size in calm bulls, reduced in chop, flat in volatile bears. Same signal, just sized to the weather.
